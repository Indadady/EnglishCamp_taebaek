PROPOSAL TO TEBAEK CITY · ENGLISH SUMMARY

Submitted by Tourmaker Co., Ltd. · 2027 Summer Season

Taebaek Global Summer School

No Quebec in Canada, Yes Taebaek in Korea

Executive Summary

This proposal invites the City of Taebaek to launch the Taebaek Global Summer School in summer 2027, using part of the summer festival budget and/or education office budget to fund a stay-based English education tourism program.

Tourmaker Co., Ltd. will serve as the official service contractor — coordinating venues, transport, lodging, meals, and festival integration. The program combines native-English curriculum with Taebaek's cool highland climate (avg. 900m) and the real Han River & Nakdong River Headwaters Festival, delivering regional promotion, temporary resident population, local economic impact, and expanded learning opportunities for local students in one package.

Year 1 (2027): pilot with 20–30 students from Taebaek and nearby coal / declining-population areas. Year 2 (2028): nationwide recruitment based on pilot data. 2029+: multi-campus expansion across Gangwon Province.

Benefits for Taebaek City

  • Brand & promotion: Position Taebaek as Korea's coolest English summer camp destination
  • Stay-based population: 1–2 week stays drive lodging, dining, and local retail — not just a few hours of festival attendance
  • Local student access: Priority enrollment for Taebaek-area students at global camp quality, without overseas camp cost
  • Repeatable city achievement: Convert one-off festival spending into a sustainable education-tourism anchor product

Program Model

Morning (academic): English classes at large local cafés, 20–30 seats each:

  • Ssuphyanggit (숲향긋) — forest-view learning lounge
  • Chorokbul (초록불) — downtown large café
  • Taebaek 875 (태백875, 세연중앞) — near Seyeon Middle School, parent-friendly access

Afternoon (activities): Linked to the Han River & Nakdong River Headwaters Festival at Hwangji Pond — water parade, kids sing-along, splash zone, night concerts, etc. Also: 365 Safety Town, coal museum, Geomnyongso forest trekking.

Evening: Cool Cinema Festival (outdoor movies), community BBQ, festival food market.

Venues & Logistics

  • Transport: Taebaek Sky Tour buses for all group movements
  • Lodging (standard): 365 Safety Town, Taebaeksan minbak village (city-linked facilities)
  • Option: 1-night camping program at a local campsite
  • Premium families: High1 Resort (Gohan–Sabuk area, near Taebaek)

Organization

  • Tourmaker Co., Ltd. — service contractor: city liaison, operations, buses, lodging, meals, festival coordination, smart guidebook. Based in Taebaek.
  • Qubi Global — proven domestic English camp SOP (Incheon Education Office camps), safety, insurance, native teacher recruitment (Korea-based freelancers)
  • Andrew Regan (Andy) — General Director: Quebec-linked curriculum, teaching quality, festival performances, parent communication, graduation certificates

Tourmaker Smart Guidebook

Every participant, parent, teacher, and staff member receives a mobile web guidebook link (Tourmaker's proven VIP tour platform):

  • Daily schedule, café classroom locations, festival map
  • Emergency contacts and on-site team directory
  • Weather, packing checklist, printable A4 schedule
  • Easy Kakao share — natural Taebaek + Tourmaker branding
