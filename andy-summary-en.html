Taebaek Global Summer School

No Quebec in Canada, Yes Taebaek in Korea — 2027 Pilot

What is this?

We propose a stay-based English summer school in Taebaek City, Gangwon Province. Taebaek is a cool highland city (avg. 900m) — similar summer climate to Quebec. The City of Taebaek uses part of its summer festival budget and/or education office budget. Tourmaker runs the program as the official contractor. You are General Director — curriculum, teaching, parents, graduation.

Year 1 (2027)

  • 20–30 students from Taebaek and nearby coal / declining-population areas
  • Build photos, data, satisfaction surveys → expand nationwide in Year 2
  • Budget from city + education office → Tourmaker service contract

Daily schedule

Morning (class): Large local cafés, 20–30 seats each:

  • Ssuphyanggit (숲향긋) — forest-view learning lounge
  • Chorokbul (초록불) — downtown large café
  • Café near Seyeon Middle School (세연중앞)

Afternoon (activities): Linked to the real Han River & Nakdong River Headwaters Festival at Hwangji Pond — water parade, kids sing-along, splash zone, night concerts, etc. Also: 365 Safety Town, coal museum, forest trekking.

Evening: Cool Cinema Festival (outdoor movies), community BBQ, festival food market.

Logistics

  • Transport: Taebaek Sky Tour buses
  • Lodging (basic): 365 Safety Town, Taebaeksan minbak village (city-linked)
  • Option: 1-night camping program at local campsite
  • Premium families: High1 Resort (Gohan-Sabuk area, near Taebaek)

Your role (Andy)

  • Lead curriculum (Quebec-linked content, PBL, presentations)
  • Teach and quality-control native English classes
  • Prepare festival performances (English musical, junior guides at festival)
  • Parent communication
  • Graduation ceremony — certificates signed by you
